About us

Welcome to our beautiful apartment complex located on the outskirts of a village in "Capilla del Monte." Nestled amidst breathtaking countryside and mountains, our establishment offers eight apartments suitable for families and young individuals. We take pride in providing a wide range of activities such as tracking, running, horse riding, and climbing, ensuring our guests experience relaxation and harmony while enjoying the fresh air and thrilling adventures available at our hotel.

At our establishment, the hotel owners have made a conscious decision to personally oversee every aspect of our operations, both in terms of maintaining the property and attending to our guests' needs. Fer is responsible for people management, ensuring our guests receive the highest level of service and care. Diego takes charge of cleaning and maintenance, ensuring that our facilities are kept in pristine condition. Seba handles administration management, ensuring the smooth operation of our establishment.

During the peak season, we hire two additional employees to assist with maintenance and cleaning duties, ensuring that our guests' comfort and satisfaction are never compromised. We are committed to providing a memorable and enjoyable stay for all who visit our hotel, and we look forward to welcoming you to our serene retreat.

Vision and Mission

Mission: Our mission is to provide a serene and rejuvenating retreat for families and young individuals at our beautiful apartment complex located on the outskirts of the village of "Capilla del Monte." We offer a range of activities such as tracking, running, horse riding, and climbing, allowing our guests to immerse themselves in relaxation, harmony, and the breathtaking natural surroundings. We are dedicated to caring for both our property and our guests, ensuring their comfort and satisfaction by managing all aspects of our operations with passion and expertise.

Vision: Our vision is to create an unforgettable experience for our guests, where they can escape the hustle and bustle of everyday life and find solace in the tranquil ambiance of our establishment. We strive to be recognized as a premier destination that offers a perfect blend of natural beauty, exciting activities, and personalized service. By maintaining our property with utmost care and dedicating ourselves to the well-being of our guests, we aim to become the preferred choice for those seeking relaxation, adventure, and harmony in the idyllic setting of "Capilla del Monte."

Rooms

Our apartment complex in the scenic outskirts of "Capilla del Monte" offers eight spacious and comfortable apartments that cater to both families and young individuals. Each room is thoughtfully designed to provide a tranquil and harmonious atmosphere, allowing our guests to relax and unwind while enjoying stunning views of the countryside and mountains. Our accommodations are perfectly suited for those seeking a blend of adventure and relaxation, as our location provides easy access to a variety of activities such as tracking, running, horse riding, and climbing. Whether you're embarking on an outdoor adventure or simply basking in the fresh air, our apartments serve as a peaceful haven to return to at the end of the day.

Living Nature

We offer a wide range of exciting activities for guests of all ages. Surrounded by stunning countryside and mountain vistas, our location is the perfect backdrop for adventure and relaxation. Guests can engage in tracking, exploring the scenic trails and discovering the natural wonders that await. If you're seeking an invigorating workout, we provide opportunities for running through the picturesque landscape. For those who have a love for horses, horse riding experiences are available, allowing you to connect with these majestic animals while enjoying the serene surroundings. For thrill-seekers and climbing enthusiasts, our establishment offers exciting opportunities to conquer the local rock formations, providing a unique and exhilarating experience. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced climber, our staff is here to assist and ensure your safety. Our focus is not just on adventure but also on relaxation and harmony. Our location provides a peaceful atmosphere where guests can immerse themselves in nature, breathe in the fresh air, and find solace in the serenity of the surroundings.
